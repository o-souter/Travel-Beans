Isle Of Lewis

The Isle of Lewis is the northern part of Lewis and Harris, the largest island of the Western Isles or Outer Hebrides archipelago in Scotland. The two parts are frequently referred to as if they were separate islands. This is the largest and northernmost island of the Outer Hebrides and is the island where the famed Lewis Chessmen were discovered. Rich in history, the Isle of Lewis is also rich in a way of life that seems most difficult to find elsewhere in the world. The Island of Lewis & Harris is on the Atlantic side of Scotland, forming the northernmost part of the Outer Hebrides. It's another favorite spot for lovers of the northern lights. You can catch a ferry from Ullapool to Lewis & Haris for a weekend of trying to see the aurora.
