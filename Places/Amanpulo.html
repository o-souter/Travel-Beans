Amanpulo

Amanpulo is located in the Philippines and is one of the best places you will ever go. It is a private island which is accessible only by a private plane. It is an island fringed by white sand beaches, the resort offers a back to nature experience in supreme comfort and privacy. For dinner you will enjoy beautiful views over the main swimming pool and out to the sea. Also this is one of the places which people from our community visit the most so if you desire to visit it before that you will have to contact us so we can make sure that there is a spot for you and also so we can make sure that you find your room for the best price available. The members of the society receive up to 30% discount depending on the season and some other bonuses.
