About Us

We have five main roles in our society. The President is the leader of the society, who coordinates and oversees all of the society’s activity. The Vice President is a key position to share the workload and help in decision making processes. Being a treasurer doesn't just mean handling money, it means knowing how your finances stand at any time, and planning accordingly. The Secretary oversees the organisation of the society and the coordination of the committee. Emails need to be written, rooms need to be booked, documents need to be completed and most importantly, you need to communicate tasks and updates between the committee, and what’s going on to your members to keep them interested and stay involved. The Social Sec should organise a wide range of socials. Reflecting the diverse nature of the student body will increase your membership and participation and everyone will feel able to get involved and make friends.
