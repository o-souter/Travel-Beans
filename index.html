About Us

Our society was created in 1995 by our main leaders Adam, Aida, Navya, Oliver and Vasil. The purpose of the society is and always was to travel around the world as a community and always to have someone to ask for an advice and help when you wish to travel somewhere new and when you want to meet new people with the same interests.

All around the world we have thousands of members in the society. Here you can find information for the best offers for travelling and also you can find people to go with. Also if you become member of the society you will have some privileges at most of the destinations and also it will be a bit cheaper for you.
