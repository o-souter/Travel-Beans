BARBADOS 2021


Trip Information

Barbados is a lush island country in the Lesser Antilles of the West Indies, in the Caribbean region of North America. It is 34 kilometres (21 miles) in length and up to 23 km (14 mi) in width, covering an area of 432 km2 (167 sq mi). It is in the western area of the North Atlantic and 100 km (62 mi) east of the Windward Islands and the Caribbean Sea; therein, Barbados is the most easterly of the Caribbean Islands. It is well known for its all year round warm weather and beautiful beaches. On July 1st 2021, we will be departing and we hope you can join us! We will be leaving from Aberdeen Airport and arriving at the Grantley Adams International Airport in Barbados. The flight will take roughly 12 hours, so be sure to bring a good book! Our yearly trips are usually divided into sections, the volunteering options in Barbados had to be taken into consideration.

As a society, we decided on helping at The Queen Elizabeth Hospital for a week, working with the staff to ease the time for the patients during our stay. Being so close with the patients allows the society members to gain a real world appreciation for the places they love travelling to. The second week of the trip will allow the members to fully immerse themselves in the culture surrounding them in Barbados. Festivals, small food carts and beautiful beaches allow the members to see the wonders of Barbados. We hope you can join us on our trip!
